About Ford Vietnam

Driving progress since 1995

Ford Vietnam was established in 1995 as a joint venture between Ford Motor Company (USA) and Sông Công Diesel Company. We are proud to be the first American automotive investor in Vietnam after normalization of diplomatic relations. With over $200 million USD in investment, our commitment remains stronger than ever.

Our operations include a state-of-the-art assembly plant in Hải Dương, 43 dealerships nationwide, and a workforce of over 1,200 employees. In 2020, we expanded our production capacity to 40,000 vehicles annually, meeting growing demand for high-quality, locally-produced Ford vehicles.

Our Vehicles

  • Ford Territory: Stylish and tech-savvy crossover launched in 2022.
  • Ford Everest: Rugged SUV with advanced Bi-turbo engine and 10-speed transmission.
  • Ford Explorer: Premium SUV imported from the US, powered by EcoBoost 2.3L.
  • Ford Ranger: Vietnam’s #1 pickup truck, including the high-performance Raptor variant.
  • Ford Transit: Leading light commercial van trusted by thousands of businesses.

Commitment to Community

At Ford Vietnam, we don’t just build cars – we build futures. Through community programs such as Driving Skills for Life, scholarships, and vehicle donations to training centers and medical institutions, we’ve impacted thousands of lives.

Inspired by Henry Ford’s vision, we continue to empower people to move freely and pursue their dreams with confidence.
